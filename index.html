Welcome to my homepage—clearly a work in progress and stubbornly unoptimized for smartphones. Perhaps you can turn your device sideways and hope for the best.

Over the last four years of my PhD, I've been busy wrestling with deep learning and machine learning, with a particular penchant for applying it in the cozy, albeit resistant confines of finance. This niche proves curiously fascinating as it lets one employ backpropagation in rather inventive ways and permits a very fine-grained dissection of information propagation at a node level.

Whenever I find a respite from the academic grind, you might catch me bouldering (a fancy word for climbing small rocks indoors), swimming (attempting not to drown stylishly), running (because the existential dread catches up when you walk), or partaking in my favorite pastimes of consuming coffee and pomegranates (the latter is as tedious as it sounds).

If you have any burning questions or simply wish to converse with someone who spends too much time contemplating neural networks, do reach out. You can also boldly download my CV, a document of no small importance to me but of questionable utility to the wider universe.

Enjoy your stay!
